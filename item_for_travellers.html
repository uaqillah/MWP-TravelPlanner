Item For Travellers

Item For Travellers

Luggage

A luggage is useful for avoiding overweight baggage fees at the airport. It allows you to weigh your luggage before your flight, ensuring it meets the airline's weight restrictions

Neck Pillow

A travel pillow provides comfort and support for your neck and head during long flights or bus/train rides. There are various types of travel pillows available, including inflatable ones and memory foam options

Portable Charger

A portable charger, also known as a power bank, is handy for charging your devices on the go. It's especially useful when you don't have access to electrical outlets or when you're traveling long distances

Adapter

A travel adapter is an essential item when traveling internationally. It allows you to plug your electronic devices into different types of power outlets found in different countries

Universal Travel Wallet

A universal travel wallet helps keep your important documents organized and secure. It typically has compartments for your passport, boarding passes, credit cards, cash, and other essentials

Small-Sized Toiletries

Travel-sized toiletries are convenient for packing and complying with airline regulations for carry-on liquids. They include items like mini shampoo, conditioner, toothpaste, and other personal care products
